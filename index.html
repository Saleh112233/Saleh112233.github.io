Recipe #1

  • Time:45min
  • Serves: 4 people
Ingredients Quantity
Pulled pork 2 lbs
Onion Powder 2 tbs
Pulled pork 4 tbs

Step 1: Grind the pork in a meat grinder.

Step 2: mix the other ingredients and spread it on the pork

Step 3:Put it in the oven for 45 mins on 450F

Enjoy these ribs with a cold coca-cola!

Recipe #2

  • Time: 30min
  • Servers : 2 People
Ingredients Quantity
Avocados 2
Greek Yogurt 3 Tsp
Salt & Pepper Pinch

Step 1: Cut the avocados in little pieces.

Step 2: Mix the yogurt and the S&P with the avocado pieces

Step 3:Store it in the fridge for 6 hours

Enjoy a healthy snack!
